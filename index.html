Account Deletion Request for Gold Mining Maniac

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data from Gold Mining Maniac, please follow the steps below:

  1. Please enter your in-game username and the registered email address associated with your account.
  2. Fill out the deletion request form down below
  3. Once your request is submitted, our support team will process your account deletion.
  4. Please note that: Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted from our active systems. For security and backup purposes, certain data may be retained in our backup systems for up to 30 days before being permanently removed.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding the deletion of your account or data, please contact us at: info.miha.games@gmail.com.
